FAQ for Visitors

What is coworking?

Coworking is about collaborating with the people around you, whether or not they’re in the same field or company. The aim is to provide a professional and social network without depending on a specific employer or company, and gaining more tools for flexibility and creative output. In short—it’s the new way to work.

How much does it cost to participate in the festival?

It is free of charge to try out the spaces! This also applies to most events that are listed in our schedule, if not you see a hint.

Which Coworking Spaces are participating in the Coworking Festival?

Our homepage has a list with photos and links to each of the spaces that are participating.

Do I need to register somewhere?

You don't need to register to visit the Coworking Festival. Some spaces will require a registration for trial days or certain events. Details can be found on the space profile on this website.

FAQ for State Ambassadors

What is a State Ambassador?

You are an important liaison between the festival organizers, participating coworking spaces, sponsors, and stakeholders in your federal state.

What are the responsibilities of a State Ambassador?

  • Reaching out to and coordinating with coworking spaces
  • Organizing a flagship event (bonus)
  • Press and communication
  • Collaborating with other ambassadors
  • Finding funding or sponsorship (bonus)

What do I need to do at a minimum to participate in the festival with my space?

Register your space and offer at least one open house day for visitors to get to know your space. Ideally, you can open your space for the entire week.

What happens after I have registered my space?

After you have registered your space, it will be listed on our website and you will be invited to the festival Slack channel. Then, we will ask you for more details about your space to fill out your space profile on the website and to use as material to introduce your space through our channels. As the festival approaches, we will ask you to inform us about your events so we can share them in our calendar and on our social media channels.

My coworking space is hosting an event for the festival, how can I share it?

We would be happy to share your event in our event calendar and on our social media channels. The requirement is that you have registered your space for the festival. We will contact you in due course after registration to get details about your event.

What are the benefits of participating?

  • Your own space profile on the Coworking Festival website, including a link and a list of your events. (Example profile from 2023)
  • PAID Social media exposure on LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook through the promotion of your space and events.
  • Possible access to the public sector.
  • Press kit.
  • Networking opportunities to increase your reach and professional coworking network.

Can you elaborate more on the idea and aim of the festival?

The Coworking Festival is a co-created one week long event that aims to raise the profile of coworking spaces in Germany, attracting new members and fostering collaboration among them. It promotes the benefits of coworking, such as professional environments, community, and work place autonomy. The festival's theme, "Work Renaissance: Shaping the Future Together," emphasizes the current transformation of work and the power of coworking to shape it. By promoting local events and spaces, the festival seeks to inspire, connect, and support coworking operators and their communities. Ultimately, it aims to strengthen the coworking sector by raising its public visibility.
